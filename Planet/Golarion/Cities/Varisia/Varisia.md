# [[Kyonin/Planet/Golarion/Cities/Varisia/Varisia]] - "*Pinnacle of Greed*"
---
- Varisia is the heart and soul of trade in Golarion, not a single copper piece enters Golarion without first passing through the [[Kyonin/Planet/Golarion/Cities/Varisia/Locations/Jasper Port/Jasper Port]]. 
- A desert city made of adobe bricks and the blood of those foolish enough to cross [[Kyonin/Planet/Golarion/Cities/Varisia/People of Varisia/Netch the Drowned]], mother of [[Planet/Golarion/Groups/Organizations/The Rat Queens Court]].
