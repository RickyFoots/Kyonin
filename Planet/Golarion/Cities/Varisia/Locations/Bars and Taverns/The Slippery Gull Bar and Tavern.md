- Sea side tavern decorated with various fishing and boating accoutrement.
- Has a wall of the local fishermen's largest catches stuffed and mounted, with the current record holder being a *Lizardfolk* man, [[Surth]], who's fishing style is highly unorthodox. 
	- He dives off his boat with a knife and swims after his prey.
	- His kill was an [[Aboleth]], roughly *26ft* in length, he caught it by diving into the creatures mouth and stabbing his knife through the rough of it's mouth.
- The go to bar for all of the local and visiting fishermen.
- Is seen as one of the most rowdy bars in town.

## Inn Keeper Name: [[Ju-Zu Adosath]]
---
1. Run by a grizzled, old *Triton* man.
